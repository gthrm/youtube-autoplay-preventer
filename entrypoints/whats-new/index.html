✦ What's New

Version 1.1.0 brings a new feature: stop YouTube from automatically playing the next video when the current one ends.

🚫

Block Autoplay After Video New

YouTube silently navigates to the next recommended video the moment your video finishes. This new toggle intercepts that navigation event and stops it — so you stay in control of what you watch next.

🖱️

Block Autoplay on Hover

Already available since v1.0.0. Prevents video thumbnails from auto-playing a preview clip when you hover over them — saving bandwidth and keeping your browsing quiet.

How to enable

1
Click the extension icon in your browser toolbar
2
Turn on Block autoplay after video in the popup
3
YouTube tabs will reload automatically — done!
